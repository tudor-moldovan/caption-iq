About me

Tudor Moldovan

I've spent over 10 years in IT — the last several of them dedicated entirely to software quality. In that time I've worked across mobile consumer apps, precision industrial systems, fintech platforms, and everything in between.

Testing, done well, is not about running scripts until the clock runs out. It's about thinking — asking the questions nobody thought to ask, exploring the corners developers didn't consider, and understanding a product well enough to know what matters most when something breaks.

I work directly with founders, engineering leads, and product teams — without layers of process in the way. You get clear findings, honest assessments, and reports your developers can act on the same day.
